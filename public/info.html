Operating principles

Empathy for creativity

Be genuinely interested in other people and imagine how their world might be better through design.

Solve 90% with 10%

Clearly articulate and intensely focus on the core problem and be relentlessly resourceful in solving it.

Build the plane on the way down

Fearlessly dive into unknowns to build the experiences that people want that don't exist yet.

Intuition then metrics

Rely on intuition from observing user behavior to build 0 to 1 experiences, then measure to double down on what’s working well.

Get ship done

Foster trust and optimize the number of experiments validated with limited time and resources by shipping 'good enough'.

Integrity all ways

Create a safe environment, listen without judgment, and do what I say I’ll do when I say I’d do it.
